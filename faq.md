# FAQ – VibeStickers

Last Updated: 2025

Below are answers to frequently asked questions about VibeStickers.

---

## ❓ Stickers are not saving to my gallery. What should I do?

Make sure:
- Storage permission is enabled  
- Your device has available space  
- You are using the latest version of the app  

---

## ❓ How do I share stickers to WhatsApp?

Open a sticker → Tap the Share icon → Select WhatsApp.

---

## ❓ Why do my stickers appear blurry?

Use high-resolution images for the best quality.  
Low-resolution images may reduce output quality.

---

## ❓ How do I delete my data?

You can delete all app data by:
- Clearing app storage  
- Uninstalling the app  

---

## ❓ The app is not working properly. What can I do?

Try:
- Restarting your device  
- Clearing cache  
- Reinstalling the app  

If the issue continues, contact us.

---

## ❓ Do you collect personal information?

No. VibeStickers does **not** collect personal user data.
